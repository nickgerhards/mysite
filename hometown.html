Harrisburg, PA

Location

Harrisburg is located slightly over 100 miles west of Philadelphia.

About

Weather

The weather is extremely unpredictable, but normally hot summers and cold winters are the expectation.

Population

Not many people live in the city anymore, with a population of just over 50,000. However, over 600,000 people call the suburbs home.

Sports

The area is home to 2 professional sports teams. The Harrisburg Senators, who are the AA minor league affiliate of the Washington Nationals, as well as the 12 time and reigning Calder Cup Champion Hershey Bears, who are the minor league affiliate of the Washington Capitals in the American Hockey League.
